Tech Consulting?

The Class Of Technical Consulting

The Class Of Technology Consulting program helps recent college graduates quickly design, develop, and deliver Oracle technology solutions in Oracle Cloud. Attendees also ensure that these solutions are delivered using Oracle's Total Cloud methodology, and adhere to best practices that have been defined from real-world consulting engagements. The program includes a mix of elearning, self-study, instructor-led workshops, and shadowing of active consulting engagements.

The Tech Consultant Role

As a member of a project team, you will follow standard practices and procedures to analyze requirements and provide quality work products to deliver functional and technical solutions for applications and technology implementations to meet customer specifications. You are expected to work directly with customers and members of the consulting delivery and sales teams on key activities.

Tech Consultant Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Information Technology, Management Information Systems or equivalent experience
  • 0-2 years of overall experience in relevant functional/technical area
  • Demonstrates competence in a technology area
  • Ability to communicate effectively and build rapport with team members and clients.
  • Exposure to, or working knowledge in certain technical and functional areas

Is Tech Consulting For You?

Are you interested in being part of a next generation solutions organization inside Oracle? Do you share the spirit of using technology to solve big problems? If you want to engineer cutting-edge solutions for our customers around Cloud Computing, Database Performance and Migrations, Modern Application Development, Oracle technologies and applications, then Oracle Class of Technical Consulting is for you.

The Tech Consulting Teams

The Austin Tech Consulting hub has seven individual teams with diverse skillsets and expertise.

Cloud Integrations Team

The Cloud Integrations Team works with the Integration Cloud Servce - app to app integration, cloud, and on-prem. This team works with the Visual Builder Cloud Service - creating UI, mobile, and web apps in a low-code environment. They also use the Process Cloud Service for Business Process Management.

Infrastructure Team

The Infrastructure Team is a team of technical architects responsible for the development & delivery of services related to Cloud Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS), as well as on-premises hardware and Engineered Systems, including assessments, architecture, implementation, and migration services.

Database Team

The Database Team works with migrations (Lift and Shift). This team also does performance tuning for long running queries. Additionally, they do health checks, upgrades, and patching (sever/database patches). The Database Team does Soar installations and uses Soar to migrate databases.

Business Intelligence Team

The Business Intelligence Team delivers Oracle BI/Analytics solutions to the customer. This team implements and provisions environments, assists with data loading and modeling, and gathers functional and business reports.

App Dev & Microservices Team

The App Dev & Microservices Team focuses on microservices and containerization. This team does Cloud Native Development. They also work with Kubernetes, FaaS, and CI/CD.

Block Chain, IOT, & App Dev Team

The Block Chain, IOT, & App Dev Team does mobile application development as well as web application development. This team encompasses UI/UX Design, Chatbots, Block Chain, and IOT.
